Hi there!

Coach and content creator
I’m Lyn, the founder of @Shit.You.Really.Need.To.Hear. I created the account during the COVID-19 pandemic. With the uncertainty of the world, my intention was to post quotes that I thought people needed to hear to get them going through their day. I developed an interest to be of service and decided to embark on my coaching journey. I shifted my career from a photographer and a year later, I am now a certified coach, encouraging people to create the life story they want for themselves.

My Mission is To..

Encourage the Next Gen to create the life story that they want for themselves & to progress in this thing called life. Because, I truly believe that we are all deserving of the life that we want for ourselves. I am here to create my own and inspire YOU to do the same. Are you ready to start creating your life story?

  • Authentic methodology

  • Accredited Alumni
